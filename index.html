About Me



Hello Dear Visitor :)

I'm Abhinav Garg. I'm someone who is really driven to make a difference. My story is a bit childish and idealistic in nature. I was someone who just used to do minimum things required and chill (actually a large part of that still remains). But in my junior year, one of my best friends was talking about Steve Jobs. I was like - "Who the hell is that?" He looked at me like I was an idiot, and I did the same to him. Then he gave me a pictorial book to read that he found in the library. Out of curiosity I flipped it over. Something snapped in me. I just knew this was awesome and had to do something like that. So, I got off my laziness and started doing 'non-minimum things.' This and that happened and here am I. See, childish and idealistic right? But guess what, I still love it. And I am gonna go through with it.

As of my interests, I'm an avid meditator. I know. You're thinking I'm copying Jobs. No, I'm not. I was into meditation before I knew who he was. Personally, I believe that meditation is super important. It gives a great insight into 'reality.' As of my other interests, I love to know more about people and have meaningful discussions. My favorite topics include philosophical and metaphysical discussions. I'm also nerdy. So, games, anime, light novels, novels, movies, tv shows, tech are high on my hobbies. I'm fine with doing outdoor-sy stuff, if that's what my friends want.

Things that I want to explore most from UHP and UC is sharpening my skills, turning fatal weaknesses into strengths, fulfill some of endeavors, and making great friends! I believe I'm doing a great job on that till now and will be doing even better in future.

If you're still reading, thanks! I appreciate it. If you have any suggestions, comments, or just general message, see contact me below!
